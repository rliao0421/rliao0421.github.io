Final Project Idea: Budget Tracker

For my final project, I plan to create a clean, simple, and intuitive budget tracker. My goal is to design a tool that makes it easy for users to input their expenses and see where their money is going. I want the interface to feel minimal but still visually engaging. One of the ideas I'm exploring is incorporating charts or visual elements uch as bar graphs, pie charts, or line charts that track spending over time. I already have a few design concepts in mind and will continue refining the layout, colors, and user flow to make the tracker both functional and enjoyable to use.
