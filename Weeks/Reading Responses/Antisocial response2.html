The film left me with an unsettling realization that the movements, memes, and conspiracies that shaped much of the past decade often arose as accidents or as part of an ongoing quest for attention and influence. The message at the end, especially from the former 4chan founder, is clear that recovering from these deep internet rabbit holes means disengaging from the chaos and taking time to reconnect with reality. While not everyone is deep into internet rabbit holes, I do believe that most of the people will benefit from spending less time on the internet and spending some time in the real world.
